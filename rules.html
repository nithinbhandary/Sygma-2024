REGISTERATION GUIDELINE

1.Registration has to be done through our website https://sygma.sdm.ac.in.

2.Registration fee of Rs. 350/- per team shall be payable ON SPOT.

3.Secure your spot by registering online and head to the College Registration Desk for the payment of the registration fee.

4.Each college is restricted to a maximum of 7 participants. Adherence to this limit is crucial during the registration process.

5.The fest is open to all the Computer Science and Computer Application students.

6. All the participants must carry and present their college ID card for verification purposes.

7.Teams have to confirm their participation via email or through call to the registration incharge Ms. Ananya M (+91 9019832272), Email- sygma@sdmcbm.ac.in

8.Teams must consist of registered participants only. Any substitutions or changes require prior approval from the organizers.

9.A team has to participate in all the events to be in the contention for the Overall Championship.

10.Participants are expected to respect and not damage any college property during the event. Any damage caused will be the financially liable.

11.The organizers reserve the right to terminate any event or disqualify participants/teams displaying vulgarity or indecency. No refunds will be issued in such cases.

12.Accuracy is Essential, Participants are accountable for providing precise registration information.

13.Refunds will not be processed after the completion of registration.

14.Strict adherence to the event schedule is imperative for smooth proceedings.

15.The deadline for registration is 6th March 2024. Please submit your registration promptly to avoid disappointment.

16.The team should report at the registration desk before 8:30 A.M.

17.For any concerns or grievances regarding online registrations, Please contact Mr. Gagan R Shetty: +91 7619637971, Mr. Jitesh S Shetty: +91 9632557581 or write us at sygma.sdmcbm.ac.in.

18.By registering, participants acknowledge and willingly commit to adhere to the Terms & Conditions outlined, ensuring a seamless and respectful event experience for all involved.
